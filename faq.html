Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about junk removal in San Antonio? We’ve got answers! Explore our FAQs or contact us for more details.

What types of items do you remove?

We remove almost anything, including furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, and construction debris. Hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, or asbestos require special handling and may not be accepted. Contact us to confirm!

How much does junk removal cost in San Antonio?

Our pricing is based on the volume of junk, starting at $99 for small loads. A quarter truck costs $199–$299, a half truck $349–$499, and a full truck $599+. We offer free quotes tailored to your needs.

Do you recycle or donate items?

Yes! As a family-owned San Antonio business, we’re committed to sustainability. We donate usable items to local charities like Habitat for Humanity and recycle materials whenever possible.

How do I schedule a pickup?

It’s easy! Use our contact form, call us at (210) 555-1234, or email info@sajunk.com. We serve areas like Alamo Heights, Stone Oak, and New Braunfels, with flexible scheduling.

Do I need to prepare my junk before you arrive?

No preparation is needed! Our team handles all the heavy lifting and sorting. Just point us to the junk, and we’ll take care of the rest, whether it’s in your San Antonio home or business.
