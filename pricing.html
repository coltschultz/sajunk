Pricing for Junk Removal

At SA Junk Removal, we believe in upfront, affordable pricing for San Antonio residents and businesses. No hidden fees, just honest service.

How We Price

Our pricing is based on the volume of junk we remove, measured by how much space it takes in our truck. We provide free, no-obligation quotes tailored to your San Antonio property, whether it’s a home in Alamo Heights or an office downtown.

  • Minimum Load: Starting at $99 for small pickups.
  • Quarter Truck: $199–$299 for light cleanouts.
  • Half Truck: $349–$499 for medium jobs.
  • Full Truck: $599+ for large cleanouts or construction debris.

Prices vary based on item type, location, and disposal requirements. Hazardous materials may incur additional fees.

What’s Included

  • Labor for loading and hauling.
  • Eco-friendly disposal, including recycling and donations.
  • Cleanup of the area after removal.
  • Friendly, professional service from our San Antonio team.

Not sure how much junk you have? We’ll assess your needs and provide a custom quote, ensuring you only pay for what we haul.
